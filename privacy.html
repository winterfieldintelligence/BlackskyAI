Privacy Policy

BlackSky AI is operated by Winterfield Intelligence Pvt Ltd. This policy explains how we collect and use data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business details: business name, category, website, location, brand voice, and audience.
  • Contact details: contact name, email, and escalation contact.
  • Platform access data: social media account IDs, tokens, and messaging templates you submit.
  • Automation preferences: posting frequency, approval mode, and workflow settings.
  • Payment evidence: UPI payment screenshots uploaded on the payment page.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Automation setup: to build and configure the workflows you request.
  • Service delivery: to run, monitor, and optimize your automations.
  • Support: to troubleshoot, maintain, and improve automation performance.

3. Data Storage

We store submitted data in secure records used for automation setup. Access is limited to the operations team and the director. We do not sell or rent your data.

4. Third‑Party Services

Your automations may connect to third‑party platforms (e.g., social networks, email providers, WhatsApp APIs). When you provide access credentials, those services may process data under their own policies.

5. Payment Verification

UPI payment screenshots are collected to verify transactions. After confirmation, payment evidence is retained for internal auditing and compliance.

6. Data Retention

We retain client data for as long as it is necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion after service completion.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to the data we store for your business.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion after service completion.

8. Contact

For privacy‑related requests, contact BlackSky AI at Winterfield Intelligence Pvt Ltd.
